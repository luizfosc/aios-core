      - dimensionalize: "Make the benefit so specific and tangible it stops sounding like hype."

    source: "[SOURCE: earlytorise.com — Four-Legged Stool]"

    source: "[SOURCE: AWAI — Neophilia Principle]"

      - urgency: "Add time-based consequence: why NOW, not later."
      - test: "Does this create PRESSURE to decide right now? If not, strengthen."
    source: "[SOURCE: AWAI — Three Advanced Techniques, Crossroads Close]"
