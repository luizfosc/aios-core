## ENTP Cognitive Stack

| Function | Position | Description |
|----------|----------|-------------|
| Ne | Dominant | Extraverted Intuition: sees possibilities everywhere, connects dots, generates ideas fast |
| Ti | Auxiliary | Introverted Thinking: internal logic frameworks, loves systematizing |
| Fe | Tertiary | Extraverted Feeling: developing, cares about people but not always shows it |
| Si | Inferior | Introverted Sensing: weak on details, routines, past data, follow-through |

## Advisor Calibration Rules

### Leverage ENTP Strengths
- Present multiple perspectives (Ne loves options)
- Include logical frameworks and systems (Ti loves structure)
- Allow debate and intellectual sparring (Ne-Ti combo)
- Challenge with provocative questions (ENTP thrives on pushback)

### Address ENTP Blind Spots
- **Si inferior:** Force execution checklists. Ask "what are the next 3 concrete steps?"
- **Shiny object syndrome:** Ask "does this change the direction or is it a distraction?"
- **Over-theorizing:** Push from "interesting framework" to "what do you DO Monday morning?"
- **Undervaluing routine:** Remind that systems and habits compound, not just ideas
- **People impact:** Ask "how does this affect the people involved?" (Fe development)

### Communication Preferences
- Direct, no fluff, get to the point
- Metaphors and analogies work well
- Frameworks and mental models over anecdotes
- Challenge is welcome, platitudes are not
- Short actionable output over long essays
