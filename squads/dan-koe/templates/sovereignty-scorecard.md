## Purpose

Standardized diagnostic template for assessing business and life sovereignty across four dimensions. Use this scorecard to identify constraints, prioritize improvements, and track progress toward full sovereignty.

---

## The Sovereignty Equation

```
                Revenue + Time + Creative + Location
  SOVEREIGNTY = ─────────────────────────────────────
                              4

  Score range: 1-10 per dimension | Overall: 1-10 average
```

**Core Principle:** Sovereignty is the goal. Money is the tool. Business is the vehicle. Life design is the destination.

## Interpretation Guide

### The Four Dimensions Explained

**Revenue Sovereignty** measures financial independence. The key question: "If you stop working for 30 days, what happens to your income?" High sovereignty means diversified, passive, and scalable revenue streams. Low sovereignty means client dependency and trading time for money.

**Time Sovereignty** measures calendar control. The key question: "Do you own your time, or does your business own it?" High sovereignty means protected deep work, full schedule control, and the ability to disappear without consequences. Low sovereignty means meetings, obligations, and reactive work.

**Creative Sovereignty** measures intellectual freedom. The key question: "Are you creating what you want, or what others demand?" High sovereignty means IP ownership, content systems, and alignment with personal obsessions. Low sovereignty means client-dictated work and no creative autonomy.

**Location Sovereignty** measures geographic freedom. The key question: "Can you work from anywhere with internet?" High sovereignty means fully digital infrastructure, no physical dependencies. Low sovereignty means office-bound, location-locked operations.

### Common Patterns

| Pattern | Diagnosis | Recommended Agent |
|---------|-----------|-------------------|
| High Revenue, Low Time | Money without freedom — need systems and automation | koe-arquiteto |
| High Creative, Low Revenue | Creating without converting — need offer architecture | koe-offers |
| High Time, Low Revenue | Freedom without income — need traffic + offers | koe-offers + koe-distribuidor |
| All Low | Fundamental restructuring — start with Revenue + Time | koe-advisor (emergency plan) |
| All High | Optimization mode — maintain and expand | koe-advisor (strategic counsel) |

### The Paradox to Remember

> "Sovereignty isn't about working less. It's about having the CHOICE to work more or less on the things that matter to you. Structure creates freedom. Systems create sovereignty."

## Frequency

- **Full Assessment:** Quarterly (every 90 days)
- **Quick Check:** Monthly (score only, no detailed audit)
- **Emergency:** Anytime a score drops below 4 on any dimension
