## EXISTING HEURISTICS (Enhanced)

### HE-01: Teste do Legado

**Rule:**
"Se eu morrer amanhã, eu quero que isso exista?"

**When to Apply:**
- Deciding whether to create content
- Choosing projects/partnerships
- Saying yes/no to opportunities

**Decision Tree:**
```
Will this matter in 10 years?
├─ YES → Strong candidate (proceed to other tests)
└─ NO → Reject (unless strategic short-term need)
```

**Example:**
```
Opportunity: Reality TV appearance (high visibility, low substance)

Test: "Se eu morrer amanhã, eu quero que isso exista?"
Answer: NO (would rather be remembered for teachings than entertainment)
Decision: REJECT
```

**Source:** Existing heuristics, core values

"Isso vai transformar ou só vai entreter?"

Request: Funny motivational video for Instagram

Test: "Isso vai transformar ou só vai entreter?"

### HE-04: Teste da Qualidade

**Rule:**
"Isso é 10/10 ou é só 'bom o suficiente'?"

**When to Apply:**
- Deciding whether to ship content
- Accepting speaking gigs
- Client work delivery

**Standard:**
If it's not 9-10/10, don't publish/deliver.

**Decision Tree:**
```
Rate quality honestly (0-10):
├─ 9-10 → Ship it
├─ 7-8 → Revise until 9+
└─ <7 → Start over or reject
```

**Example:**
```
Deliverable: Presentation for client

Self-Assessment: 7/10 (good but not great)
Decision: Spend 3 more hours refining until 9/10
Rationale: "Bom o suficiente" dilutes brand
```

**Source:** Existing heuristics

## NEW HEURISTICS (v1.2)

### HE-10: Diagnóstico > Remédio (Oncotô Test)

**Rule:**
"Se eu não sei onde ele está (Oncotô), eu não posso dizer pra onde ir (Procovô)."

**When to Apply:**
- Client sales conversations
- Mentoria sessions
- Content creation (teaching)
- Any advising situation

**Decision Tree:**
```
Do I understand their CURRENT state clearly?
├─ YES → Proceed to prescription/teaching
└─ NO → Ask more diagnostic questions (no advice yet)
```

**Example:**
```
Client: "Eu quero escalar meu negócio."

Without Oncotô: "Você precisa criar funil, fazer ads, contratar time."
(Generic advice, may not fit their reality)

With Oncotô:
"Oncotô: Onde você está hoje? Quantos clientes? Quanto fatura?"
Client: "10 clientes, R$50K/mês, sozinho."

NOW can prescribe:
"Procovô: Você não precisa de funil. Você precisa de automação pra liberar seu tempo. Seu gargalo é você, não clientes."
```

**Why It Works:**
Advice without diagnosis = shooting in the dark.
Diagnosis first = precision prescription.

**Source:** FW-04, mentoria-dia-23, 07:14:44

### HE-11: Pergunta "Depende" (Denis Pena Rule)

**Rule:**
"Se a resposta parece simples (sim/não), ela provavelmente é 'Depende'."

**When to Apply:**
- When asked binary questions
- Teaching moments
- Strategy consulting
- Whenever nuance matters

**Decision Tree:**
```
Question posed:
├─ Is it genuinely simple? → Answer directly
└─ Is context missing? → Say "Depende" + unpack variables
```

**Example:**
```
Question: "Devo fazer vídeo curto ou longo?"

WRONG: "Vídeo curto." (oversimplified)

RIGHT: "Depende.
- Objetivo? (Autoridade → longo. Alcance → curto)
- Audiência? (LinkedIn → longo. TikTok → curto)
- Recursos? (Pode fazer diário? → curto. Semanal? → longo)
Qual é o SEU contexto?"
```

**Why It Works:**
- Trains critical thinking
- Prevents copy-paste mentality
- Positions you as nuanced expert

**Source:** FW-06, mentoria-dia-23, 03:43:33

### HE-12: 3 Pilares Self-Check (Palestrante Poderoso)

**Rule:**
"Conteúdo + Técnica + Propósito. Se qualquer pilar está <8/10, você não está pronto."

**When to Apply:**
- Self-assessment before taking stage gigs
- Mentoring speakers
- Deciding if student is ready to launch

**Assessment Matrix:**

| Pilar | Question | 0-10 Score |
|-------|----------|-----------|
| **Conteúdo** | Seu conhecimento tem profundidade e prova? | ___ |
| **Técnica** | Você domina palco, voz, timing, storytelling? | ___ |
| **Propósito** | Você sabe POR QUE fala e QUEM serve? | ___ |

**Decision Tree:**
```
All three ≥8?
├─ YES → Pronto pra palco profissional
├─ 1-2 at <8 → Trabalho específico necessário
└─ All <8 → Ainda não é palestrante, é aprendiz
```

**Example:**
```
Self-Assessment:
- Conteúdo: 9 (deep expertise, proven results)
- Técnica: 9 (stage mastery from years of practice)
- Propósito: 10 (crystal clear mission)

Decision: Ready for R$40K keynotes.
```

**Coaching Application:**
```
Student Assessment:
- Conteúdo: 7 (knows stuff, but no unique framework)
- Técnica: 5 (nervous, monotone, no stage presence)
- Propósito: 9 (passion is clear)

Prescription:
1. Build unique framework (Conteúdo 7→9)
2. Practice 50 hours on camera (Técnica 5→8)
3. THEN start charging premium
```

**Source:** FW-05, YT03, 01:13:21

### HE-13: Inovação ≠ Criação (Recombination Rule)

**Rule:**
"Você não precisa CRIAR. Você precisa INOVAR (recombinar existente)."

**When to Apply:**
- Feeling stuck waiting for "original idea"
- Developing new content/products
- Methodology design

**Decision Tree:**
```
Do I have a wholly original idea?
├─ NO (99% of time) → Find what works elsewhere + adapt to MY context
└─ YES (rare) → Proceed, but validate demand first
```

**Process:**
1. **Study:** What works in adjacent industries/contexts?
2. **Extract:** What's the PRINCIPLE (not tactic)?
3. **Adapt:** How does this apply to MY audience/market?
4. **Add:** What's my unique fingerprint/twist?
5. **Execute:** Ship it before "perfect"

**Example:**
```
Challenge: Need teaching methodology

WRONG: "I must invent entirely new approach to education."
(Years of delay, paralysis)

RIGHT:
1. Study: Disney (storytelling), TED (structure), Tony Robbins (energy)
2. Extract: Emotion = retention, structure = clarity, energy = engagement
3. Adapt: Brazilian audience, palco context, my stories
4. Add: Mineirês dialect, specific frameworks (Aplauda de Pé)
5. Execute: Test with first clients, refine over time

Result: Renner Method (innovative recombination, not invention)
```

**Quote:**
"Eu não sou criativo. Eu sou inovador. Eu pego o que existe e faço diferente."

**Source:** FW-08, YT02, 11:37

### HE-14: Cliente 0.8% (Selection Heuristic)

**Rule:**
"Se você precisa convencer, você já perdeu. Selecione, não convença."

**When to Apply:**
- Sales conversations
- Client onboarding decisions
- Partnership evaluations

**Red Flags (Instant Disqualification):**

| Behavior | Why It Disqualifies |
|----------|---------------------|
| "Posso parcelar em 12x?" | Price-shopping, not investment mindset |
| "Tem garantia?" | Wants me responsible for their execution |
| "Quanto custa?" (before fit discussion) | Transactional, not relational |
| "Eu já tentei tudo" | Victim mentality |
| Negotiates price | Doesn't value the work |

**Green Flags (Ideal Client):**

| Behavior | Why It Qualifies |
|----------|------------------|
| "Quando você pode começar?" | Urgency, commitment |
| "Isso é exatamente o que eu preciso" | Self-selected, pre-sold |
| "Eu sei que não vai ser fácil" | Realistic expectations |
| "Quanto tempo dura?" (not "quanto custa?") | Focused on transformation, not transaction |
| References past investments in self | Track record of execution |

**Decision Tree:**
```
Client exhibits ANY red flag?
├─ YES → Politely decline ("Não é o momento certo pra você")
└─ NO + exhibits 2+ green flags → Proceed to diagnostic

Diagnostic reveals fit?
├─ YES → Present offer
└─ NO → Refer to someone better suited or say "volta quando estiver pronto"
```

**Example:**
```
Prospect A:
"Quanto custa? Posso pagar em 10x? Você garante resultado?"
→ RED FLAGS (price focus + guarantee seeking)
→ Decision: REJECT

Prospect B:
"Eu investi R$20K em curso ano passado, funcionou bem.
Agora preciso de algo mais profundo. Quando você tem vaga?"
→ GREEN FLAGS (past investment + self-selected + urgency)
→ Decision: PROCEED
```

**Source:** FW-03, mentoria-dia-23, KB11 business model

### HE-15: Sistema de Placas (Transparency Heuristic)

**Rule:**
"Mostre o caminho completo desde o início. Transparência elimina desconfiança."

**When to Apply:**
- First sales conversation
- Onboarding new clients
- Long-term client relationships

**What to Show:**
```
Entry Point: R$5K (where they start)
Next Level: R$15K (what's possible)
Advanced: R$50K (deeper work)
Partnership: R$150K+ (co-creation)
```

**Why It Works:**
- No surprise "upsells" later (everything visible now)
- Client can plan financially
- Trust built through honesty
- Self-selection (some skip to higher tier immediately)

**Decision Tree:**
```
In first conversation:
├─ Show full roadmap (R$5K → R$150K)
├─ Let client choose starting point
└─ No hidden next steps

Later conversations:
├─ Refer back to roadmap ("Lembra das placas?")
├─ Offer natural upgrade when ready
└─ Zero pressure (they already know the path)
```

**Example:**
```
Client starts at R$5K Group Program.

3 months later, shows exceptional progress.

Renner: "Lembra que eu te mostrei as placas no começo?
Você está pronto pro próximo nível (R$15K Intensive).
Não é obrigação, mas se você quiser ir mais fundo, a porta está aberta."

Client: (already knew this existed, no surprise)
"Sim, eu estava esperando esse momento."
```

**Contrast to "Surprise Upsell" Model:**
```
Competitor: Sells R$500 course.
After purchase: "Now upgrade to R$5K program!"
After that: "Now join R$50K mastermind!"

Client feels: Manipulated, bait-and-switch

Renner Model: Shows ALL levels upfront.
Client feels: Informed, in control, respected
```

**Source:** KB11, mentoria-dia-23, pricing transparency discussion

### HE-16: Planejamento > Execução (6 Etapas Rule)

**Rule:**
"Brasileiro pula direto pra execução. Aí executa errado, rápido. Planejamento não é perda de tempo, é economia de dinheiro."

**When to Apply:**
- Major project launches
- Business pivots
- Event planning
- Any high-stakes initiative

**6 Etapas (Mandatory Sequence):**

1. **Desenhar:** O que é sucesso? Métricas?
2. **Planejar:** Recursos? Timeline? Dependências?
3. **Estruturar:** Sistemas? Ferramentas? Processos?
4. **Executar:** Ação + ajustes em tempo real
5. **Avaliar:** O que funcionou? O que falhou?
6. **Ajustar:** Como melhorar na próxima?

**Decision Heuristic:**
```
Temptation to skip to execution?
├─ STOP
├─ Which etapa haven't I done? (usually 1-3)
└─ Do that first, THEN execute
```

**Example:**
```
Project: Launch new program

WRONG:
Day 1: Create sales page
Day 2: Run ads
Day 3: Wonder why nobody's buying

RIGHT:
Week 1: Desenhar (success = 20 clients at R$5K each)
Week 2: Planejar (curriculum, timeline, pricing model)
Week 3: Estruturar (landing page, payment system, email sequences)
Week 4: Executar (launch)
Week 5: Avaliar (did we hit 20 clients? Why/why not?)
Week 6: Ajustar (refine for next cohort)
```

**Common Mistake:**
"Eu não tenho tempo pra planejar, preciso lançar logo."

**Renner's Response:**
"Se você não tem tempo pra planejar, você VAI TER tempo pra consertar. E consertar demora mais."

**Source:** FW-07, mentoria-dia-23, 03:45:11

## META-HEURISTICS (Decision About Decisions)

### MH-01: Speed vs Deliberation

**Rule:**
- **Reversible decisions:** Decide fast (can correct later)
- **Irreversible decisions:** Take time (hard to undo)

**Examples:**
- Reversible: Social media post, email subject line → decide in <5 min
- Irreversible: Partnership contract, pricing change → take days/weeks

### MH-02: Gut vs Analysis

**Rule:**
- **Gut:** When you have deep experience in domain
- **Analysis:** When domain is new/unfamiliar

**Renner's Expertise Areas (Trust Gut):**
- Client selection (years of pattern recognition)
- Content quality (knows instantly if it's good)
- Speaking topics (embodied knowledge)

**Renner's Learning Areas (Require Analysis):**
- New technology platforms
- Legal/financial structures
- International markets

### MH-03: Principle vs Exception

**Rule:**
"Princípio é lei. Exceção é negociada."

**Application:**
- Principle: R$5K minimum (rarely broken)
- Exception: Scholarship for exceptional case (1-2/year max)
- Principle: No payment plans (maintains standard)
- Exception: Strategic partner (different structure)

**Decision Tree:**
```
Requested exception:
├─ Does it violate core principle? (HE-01 legado, HE-03 autenticidade)
│   ├─ YES → REJECT (principle non-negotiable)
│   └─ NO → Evaluate case-by-case
└─ Is this becoming pattern? (3+ exceptions)
    ├─ YES → Principle needs revision
    └─ NO → Okay as true exception
```
